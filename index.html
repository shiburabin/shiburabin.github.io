About Me

I am a PhD student in Theoretical Physics at IMSc, Chennai, working on the physics of intrinsically disordered proteins (IDPs), polyampholytes, and biomolecular phase separation. I develop coarse-grained models and use molecular simulations to understand sequence-function relationships in disordered proteins.

Research Interests

  • Polymer physics of disordered proteins
  • Phase separation (LLPS) in biological systems
  • Coarse-grained molecular dynamics
  • Charge patterning and sequence-encoded function
